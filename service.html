Features included in City Ride

User-friendly Interface

City Ride offers an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, making it simple for riders and drivers to navigate the app.

Real-time Ride Tracking

Users can track their ride in real-time, providing transparency and ensuring a smoother and safer journey the ride.

Feature heading

City Ride supports of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital, and cash, catering to all preferences.

Ride Scheduling

With City Ride, users can schedule rides in advance, ensuring they have a ride ready whenever they need it.

Ride Scheduling

Driver Ratings

Driver Verification

24/7 Customer Support

City Ride offers round-the-clock customer support to assist users with any issues or inquiries, ensuring a smooth experience anytime.
